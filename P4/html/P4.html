Testing Document:

HER SKAL VI SKRIVE NOE OM TESTINGEN VI GJENNOMFØRERThe purpose of the website is to create a closer link between the band and their fans. To intrigue new followers and create a lager fan base. The web site will also supply fans with music and products to increase the Hellmist Dragonfire fever that it gripping the world. The site also supply the fans with information about that they have and are doing in terms of day to day and big tours. Our audience is new fans and old fans; people how have been to a concert or just heard a song they liked and want to get to know the band. Their fans are mainly from Canada, USA and Western Europe, even though there is quite a large fan group forming in South-Korea at the moment. Their ages vary a lot as well, the youngest fans being about 15 years old, while the oldest are in their 60s. However, they all have rock music and Hellmist Dragonfire in common. Thus, the website needs to be user friendly, because our target demographic consists of people who may not be used to websites and internet. Agents will also find contact information and a first impression if they are looking to book the band for different gigs.

Tester Audience:

Our page is is meant for a diverse demographic and must answer the demands of our technology savvy younger crowd while still being so intuitive that our older fans don’t get frustrated by all the complex shortcuts and icons. There were a challenge reaching the older demographics and for this survey this demographics is represented by our different families. This of course is an issue for the survey as our families has a somewhat higher education that our target demographics and also families can bee byes. As for the younger crowd that Hellmist dragonfire is trying to reach with this page we have been able to reach alot more in this target demographics. of course we have to keep in mind that a lot of our subjects are students. In the end We were able to reach 36 subjects in total. of them 28 were students at NTNU and 8 were family members; 6 parents and 2 uncles.
