Administrative Details

Our client is the rising band, Hellmist Dragonfire. The band consists of five members and has been active for 5 years, but have yet to see the wave of fame. The band consists of one vocalist, Jane Javelin, the next door girl from Dallas, Texas. At 54 years of age, her biggest wish is to become a popstar like Britney Spears and perform in Madison Square Garden. The drummer’s name is Randy - also known as Randolph Gregorian the third. Randy’s highest ambition is fame. Harrison and Morrison Horde are the twins who play guitar and bass. These shy boys are only in the band to make enough money so that they can open their own winery in Tuscany. Hellmist Dragonfire is on the rise, and their mutual goal to become famous drives their motivation. The band has realized that the only way to be a successful musician in the current day and age is to have a strong and well calculated internet presence.

Purpose and goals

The goal of the website is to promote their upcoming tours, sell merchandise and give their fans easy access to information about albums and singles, present and future. The band wants the fans to be able to look back on previous tours to get them excited about the next one. This is achieved through an gallery tab on the webpage. They also want the information about the upcoming tours to be readily available on the site. In addition to promoting the bands activities they also want to give the fans a chance to see a more personal side of the band members. This is done through our bios tab, this is updated daily. To get people even more excited about their music the band also wants there to be a tab that promotes the albums and singles they have released.

Audience

The typical user of this website will be the average Hellmist Dragonfire fan. Hellmist Dragonfire has fans all over the world, and thus the website needs to appeal to a set of different people from different cultures. Also, these fans are not know to be the sharpest knives, which means the website needs to be very user friendly. The user will likely want to know dates of tours and when the new album will be released as well as a way to get to know the members of the band better. Also, if something is unclear, they will want contact info for where they shall send their questions.

Content and organized

  • Home

    • This page is a simple page, telling you what the site contains and gives you an intuitive feel for how you navigate the page. It will be mostly a visual page, with easily identifiable links to all other pages and as little unnecessary text as possible.

  • Gallery

    • If you are a new fan or just an old fan looking to relive glory days. This gallery contains selected pictures from tours and concerts. Telling the story of all the highlights throughout the bands history.

  • About

    • This section will provide a short description on the lives of each member in the band, and the band as a whole. The page will be updated with pictures and short texts about life on the road, by the band on a day to day basis.

  • Shop/Merch

    • This is a pretty traditional webshop. It will carry items that the band are selling, mostly consisiting of t-shirts and hats, that fans can buy to prove their devotion. Naturally this is a big source of income for the band, so the page should be easily navigated and display the products in a clear and straight forward manner. Each product will have its own product page with a short description and some items will have different size options.

  • Music

    • This is meant for old fans to easily find their favorites and for new listeners to explore the variety of music made by the band. The bands newest music will be listed first, as well as where to stream or buy it. This section could possibly link to the Shop/Merch page, which would sell music in different formats.

  • Tour information

    • To ensure that people know where the band is playing. The page is guaranteed to be up to date and it will list future tour stops chronologically (from first to last) so this is the place to go if you are in doubt. The various stops will contain a date, a city, festival/[] info and a link to relevant pages (where to buy tickets for instance).
