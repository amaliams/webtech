Administrative Details

Our client is the rising band, Hellmist Dragonfire. They have been active for about 5 years, but have yet to see the wave of fame. The band consists of one vocalist, Jane Javelin, the next door girl from Dallas, Texas. At 54 years of age, her biggest wish is to become a popstar like Britney Spears and perform in Madison Square Garden. The drummer’s name is Randy - also known as Randolph Gregorian the third. Randy’s highest ambition is to be surrounded by girls. Previously, Randy has had a questionable reputation, mostly because of his STDs, which keep returning - much to Randy’s surprise. Harrison and Morrison Horde are the twins who play guitar and bass. These shy boys are only in the band to make enough money so that they can open their own winery in Tuscany. Hellmist Dragonfire is on the rise, and their mutual goal to become famous gives them an unusual spirit and great motivation.

Purpose and goals

The goal of the website is to promote their upcoming tours and give their fans easy access to information about their albums and singles. The band wants the fans to be able to look back on previous tours to get them excited about the next one. This is achieved through an gallery tab on the webpage.They also want the information about the upcoming tours to be readily available on the site. In addition to promoting the bands activities they also want to give the fans a chance to see a more personal side of the band members. This is done through our bios tab, this is updated daily. To get people even more excited about their music the band also wants there to be a tab that promotes the albums and singles they have released.

Audience

The typical user of this website will be the average Hellmist Dragonfire fan. Hellmist Dragonfire has fans all over the world, and thus the website needs to appeal to a set of different people from different cultures. Also, these fans are not know to be the sharpest knives, which means the website needs to be very user friendly. The user will likely want to know dates of tours and when the new album will be released as well as a way to get to know the members of the band better. Also, if something is unclear, they will want contact info for where they shall send their questions.
